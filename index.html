Our mission is to provide everyone with the opportunity to own a home - We do this by offering built-to-order tiny homes.

Why Choose a Scaffold Tiny Home?

The tiny house movement is growing rapidly as people seek a simpler, more intentional lifestyle. The benefits of any traditional tiny house are well established...

  • Lower Cost: Smaller size means reduced construction, maintenance, and utility costs. Heating is more efficient, with less energy needed to maintain a comfortable temperature, leading to long-term savings.
  • Smaller Footprint: Tiny homes require fewer materials, consume less energy, and leave a smaller footprint, making them more sustainable. Eco-friendly materials and off-grid options can further reduce your carbon footprint.
  • Increased Mobility: Whether on wheels or a permanent foundation, a tiny home offers unmatched freedom. Itâ€™s perfect for those wanting to explore new locations or live closer to nature.
  • A Simpler Existence: Minimalism forces thoughtful organization and prioritizing experiences over possessions, resulting in a less cluttered, more mindful life focused on what truly matters.

Scaffold Tiny Homes greatly emphasize these strengths. We do this by digitally modelling all homes in 3D-CAD software - down to the last nail - such that the model ultimately contains absolutely all information about the build. This provide us, and therefore you, with a set of additional advantages...

  • Better Designs: We can rapidly iterate on designs to determine the ideal combination of components that will form the perfect home without investing real-world materials.
  • Better Planning: We can precisely plan out the optimal schedule for every cut, brush stroke and swing of the hammer, eliminating wasted effort and misunderstandings, while enabling parallellized construction.
  • Complete Optimization: We can use custom software to fully optimize the use of stock material that come in standard dimensions, such as timber beams, plywood sheets, batts insulation, wiring etc, eliminating waste.
  • Accurate estimates: We know the exact bill-of-materials, as well as the volume and mass of every component, prior to construction, which enables estimates that are decimal-accurate.
  • Absolute Repairability: We can provide a fully detailed and illustrated manual that allows the home owner to do their own repairs, modifications and upgrades - Having a 3D model is functionally equivalent to having x-ray vision.
